[Event] works in four simple steps.

1-Locate:

your gateway to connect with events around your area [Events] works in four simple steps.


2-Confirm or create:

It's simple, on [Events] homepage, enter the name of the location you want to
spend some good quality time with people and click on search.


3-Go!

Go to check the events nearby the location you searched for and apply for any [Events] available. Every [gathering] has
a subject, that you can search for too! If no [Events] are available on the location you selected, you can create and share your event.


4-Have a good time:

Select the [Events], wait for the scheduled day and go! Be prepared to meet new people,
create new connections and have a great time!
